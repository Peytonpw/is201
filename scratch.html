A LITTLE ABOUT THROWING

  1. Shotput
    • Shotput is a event with two techniques
    • The First technique is called the glide which is done by propelling yourself in a straight line then rotating 180 degrees to throw the implement
    • The Second is the spin where you spin 360 degrees before lkanding in a position of power, then you rotate 180 degrees to throw the implement
    • The shotput is held on the neck until it is thrown
  2. Discus
    • Unlike Shotput, the discus has one Technique, the spin
    • The spin is done similarly to shotput, spinning in 360 degrees before landing in the position of power then rotating 180 degrees to throw.
    • The Discus is held away from the body on the fingertips until thrown
